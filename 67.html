67

Origin

Kid known as the 6'7 kid now, went to a basketball game where Michael Jordan played, and his friend started to film a video, the 6'7 kid shouted "SIX SEVEEEEEEEEEEEN!!" which is Michael Jordan's height

Meaning

The height of Michael Jordan
